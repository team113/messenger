Rx<RxStatus> status

Status of the messages fetching.

May be:

  • status.isEmpty, meaning messages were not yet initialized.
  • status.isLoading, meaning messages are being loaded from the local storage.
  • status.isSuccess, meaning messages were successfully initialized with local data and are ready to be used.
  • status.isLoadingMore, meaning messages are being fetched from the service.
